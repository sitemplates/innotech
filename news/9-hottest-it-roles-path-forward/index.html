9 hottest IT roles that may be your best path forward

June 14, 2021 — Articles, Blog, Talents

Either if you are just starting your career or considering a change in your initial plans, it’s always a good idea to know the options before moving forward.

By 2030, there will be a global shortage of more than 85 million tech workers, according to the management consulting firm Korn Ferry. Job candidates for the most in-demand IT roles are hard to find and highly valued, so this is definitely a career to invest in. But which way to go? Either if you are just starting or considering a change in your initial plans, it’s always a good idea to know the options before moving forward.

Here are some rewarding and future-proof IT roles you should think about:

Systems, Network, Data and Cloud Security Professionals

IT Security professionals are one of the most sought-after jobs as businesses increasingly rely on data for everyday business operations. Systems, Network, Data and Cloud Security roles are critical to making sure the company’s info remains safe from potential threats, whether they come from inside or outside. Knowledge of IAM (Identity and Access Management Compliance), PAM (Privileged Access Management), SIEM (Security Information and Event Management), regulations and top trends is highly valued.

DevOps Engineer

DevOps is a software development strategy that bridges the gap between developers and IT staff. It is the result of applying the agile paradigm to the entire service lifecycle from conception to production support. It requires the involvement of the development and operations engineers in all steps of the process and thus generalizing the automation efforts that previously were only seen during the development phases. The DevOps Engineer is a professional that introduces processes, tools, and methodologies and works with developers and IT staff to oversee the code releases. Responsibilities go from coding and deployment to maintenance and updates.

Cloud Architect

We are in a world dominated by cloud computing. That’s what enables doing everything from anywhere and democratizes access to the best technologies. Cloud Architects work on incorporating cloud adoption, cloud application design as well as cloud management and monitoring. They are responsible for converting the technical requirements of a project into the architecture and design that will guide the final product. Knowledge of state-of-the-art cloud technologies and architectural principles, troubleshooting and debugging is highly valued.

Quality Assurance Engineer

Typically defined as a procedure to ensure the quality of software products or services provided by an organization, Quality Assurance focuses on improving the software development process and making it efficient and effective based on the quality standards defined. QA is to test what DevOps is to automation. Knowledge of quality assurance systems and ecosystems, modern agile quality assurance methodologies, test automation at scale and cloud technologies is highly valued.

Data Scientist/ Analyst

As we all know, data is the new oil and Data Science and Data Analyst IT roles are truly rocking the market to help companies transforming raw info into valuable insights. Data Analysts sift through data and seek to identify trends, discovering what stories do the numbers tell and what decisions should be made based on that. Data Scientists are pros at interpreting data, but also tend to have coding and mathematical modeling expertise. Most Data Scientists can do the work of a Data Analyst but are also hands-on in machine learning, statistics, data mining, data warehousing techniques, or schema design.

Project Manager

In 2027, employers will need 87.7 million individuals working in project management-oriented IT roles. And so there is no doubt that the job outlook for professionals with project management skills is extremely positive. In the broadest sense, Project Managers are responsible for planning, organizing, and directing the completion of specific projects. At the same time, they also have to ensure these projects are on time, on budget and within scope. Knowledge of Project Management (PMI) principles, concepts, procedures, practices and tools is highly valued.

Software Developer

Software applications have become an integral part of our life, and the demand for people who build them has never been greater. The name says it all. Software Developers develop software applications using a variety of programming languages. According to the PopularitY of Programming Language Index, created by analyzing how often language tutorials are searched on Google, the trendiest at the moment are Python, Java, JavaScript, C#, C/C++, PHP and R. If you are betting on this career path, take it into consideration. Also, there are different IT roles here, and each one solves specific problems and has domain-specific knowledge, such as Front-end Developers, Back-end Developers, Full-stack Developers, Mobile Developers, Web Developers…

Blockchain Engineer

With blockchain slowly taking over the market, there’s a lot of demand for the proper workforce that can fuel the system. Initially created as a platform for supporting bitcoin, Blockchain is demonstrating a level of versatility and security that has made many sectors of business and government take notice and begin putting it to their use. Blockchain engineers implement and create a digital blockchain for corporate solutions using platforms like Hyperledger and Ethereum, designing the security and the architecture of the proposed system. Knowledge of data structures, cryptography, smart contracts, computer networking, and algorithms is highly valued.

IoT Engineer

The primary task of IoT Engineers is the creation and development of the devices, sensors and software that allows them to connect seamlessly with other systems. They research, create, test and document IoT solutions with integrated systems and devices, combining three main elements: data, technology and research. Knowledge of machine learning and Artificial Intelligence, JavaScript and Python, Node.js, big data, security, GPS systems, cloud computing and mobile hardware environment is highly valued.
